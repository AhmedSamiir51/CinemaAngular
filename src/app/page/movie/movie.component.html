THE LOST CITY

Coming Soon to Your Cinema (Sample Site)

120 mins | Rated PG-13 (for violence and some bloody images, suggestive material, partial nudity and language. ) | Action Directed by Adam Nee, Aaron Nee | Starring Oscar Nuñez, Patti Harrison, Daniel Radcliffe, Brad Pitt, Sandra Bullock, Channing Tatum

Brilliant, but reclusive author Loretta Sage (Sandra Bullock) has spent her career writing about exotic places in her popular romance-adventure novels featuring handsome cover model Alan (Channing Tatum), who has dedicated his life to embodying the hero character, “Dash.” While on tour promoting her new book with Alan, Loretta is kidnapped by an eccentric billionaire (Daniel Radcliffe) who hopes that she can lead him to the ancient lost city’s treasure from her latest story. Wanting to prove that he can be a hero in real life and not just on the pages of her books, Alan sets off to rescue her. Thrust into an epic jungle adventure, the unlikely pair will need to work together to survive the elements and find the ancient treasure before it’s lost forever.
